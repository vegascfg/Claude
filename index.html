Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Chat?
AI Chat is a unified platform that gives you access to the world's leading AI models in one place. Instead of juggling multiple accounts and apps, you can chat with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Grok, and more from a single interface.
Which AI models are available?
We support models from OpenAI (GPT-5, GPT-4o), Anthropic (Claude 4 Sonnet, Claude 3.7 Sonnet), Google (Gemini 2.5 Pro, Gemini 2.5 Flash), DeepSeek (R1), xAI (Grok 3), and more. New models are added within days of their public release.
Is AI Chat free to use?
Yes, you can start chatting for free with a generous daily allowance. For heavy usage or access to premium models like GPT-5 and Claude 4, we offer affordable subscription plans.
How do I choose the right AI model?
Each model has different strengths. GPT-5 is best for general-purpose tasks and creative work. Claude excels at long document analysis, coding, and nuanced writing. Gemini is strongest at multimodal tasks involving images and video. DeepSeek R1 leads in mathematical reasoning. You can try multiple models on the same prompt to compare responses.
Can I compare responses from different AI models?
Absolutely. You can send the same prompt to multiple models and see their responses side by side. This is one of the most powerful features of AI Chat — it helps you find which model works best for your specific use case.
Is my data safe and private?
Yes. All conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never use your conversations to train AI models. You can export or delete your entire chat history at any time. We comply with GDPR and major privacy regulations.
Do I need to create separate accounts for each AI provider?
No. That's the main advantage of AI Chat. You create one account and get access to all supported AI models. No need to manage multiple subscriptions or remember different login credentials.
What types of tasks can I use AI Chat for?
Almost anything: writing emails and articles, generating and debugging code, analyzing documents, brainstorming ideas, learning new topics, translating languages, creating content, solving math problems, and much more. Different models excel at different tasks.
